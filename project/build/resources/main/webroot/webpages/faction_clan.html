Welcome, brave warrior.

Everything you need to know about your faction

This faction was created a few hundred years ago by the followers of the great dragon, Aldruïn They served Aldruïn until mankind slaughtered every last dragon in the world. Now, there sole purpose is to build and protect statues and castles in name of Aldruïn and the other killed dragons.

The faction exists of men and women. They are a large group spread across the entire world. They give their knowledge from parent to child.

But humankind is not the same as hundreds of years ago. People have stopped believing in the existence of dragons. So the number of people in this faction has dropped immensely over the years. They need members like you to spread the word and prove that dragons have existed.
